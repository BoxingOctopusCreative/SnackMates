Boxing Octopus Creative ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the SnackMates website and related services (the "Service").

## 1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

- Account details such as your name, email address, username, and password
- Profile information such as your bio, avatar, and banner images
- Wishlist content, messages, friend connections, and other activity within the Service
- Support requests or other communications you send to us

We also collect limited technical information automatically, such as device type, browser, IP address, and usage data needed to secure and improve the Service.

## 2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and maintain your account
- Provide wishlists, messaging, matching, and other core features
- Send account-related emails such as verification and password reset messages
- Monitor for abuse, fraud, and security incidents
- Improve and operate the Service

We do not sell your personal information.

## 3. How we share information

We may share information:

- With service providers that help us host, operate, or support the Service
- When required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and SnackMates

Other users may see information you choose to make visible on your profile, wishlists, or messages.

## 4. Cookies and local storage

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences such as theme selection, and understand how the Service is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

## 5. Data retention

We retain account and activity information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

## 6. Your choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal information we hold about you. You can update much of your profile information in account settings. For other requests, contact us using the email below.

## 7. Children's privacy

SnackMates is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

## 8. International users

If you access the Service from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.
